Who am I?

I am an agile freelance developer who leads through example and by getting things done; I provide my services to a number of companies across Europe and India and have done for some time.

  • I write code either remote or with your team
  • I help with emergencies
  • I provide guidance over architecture and testing
  • I am available for workshops and speaking engagements
  • I work with C#, JS, RavenDB, Node.js and Clojure
  • I make everybody happy
